## Premise
I have found myself frustrated when I go to the fitness center at National Taiwan University and every machine, bench, and rack is in use. Luckily, the flow of people and occupancy is posted online on `https://rent.pe.ntu.edu.tw/` and is updated whenever you reload the page. 

My goal was to identify tims where gym occupancy is most optimal during the week, and perhaps run the script throughout a semester to identify trends by week. 

Note: data is only collected in the below time periods (ending 30 minutes before gym close)
- Mon-Fri: 08:00-21:30
- Sat: 09:00-21:30
- Sun: 09:00-17:30
